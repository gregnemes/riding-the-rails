Unlike most Chines HSR stations, Guangzhou South Railway Station is partially accessible to non-ticketed passengers. Source: Archdaily.
The non-public portions of the station are "lifted," leaving the ground level shops and cafes accessible to the general public. Source: Author.
The public can freely circulate at ground level. Source: Author.
