Before Sunrise: Jesse and Celine meet on a train in Europe, and wind up spending one romantic evening together in Vienna.
North by Northwest: A classic Hitchcock suspense film. On the run from the police, Roger meets Eve aboard a train to Chicago.
Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind: Joel and Clementine meet (for the second time) on the Long Island Railroad from Montauk.
The Lady Vanishes: Another Hitchcock suspense film. Lady Froy convinces Gilbert to help her search for a missing passenger aboard a train through the Swiss Alps.
Densha Otaku: The supposedly true story of a 23-year-old geek who intervenes when a drunk man harasses several women on a Japanese train.
Shanghai Express: A famous cabaret singer, Shanghai Lil, runs into her former lover Donald on a dangerous train ride to Shanghai during a civil war.
Le Train: When Julien is separated from his wife and daughter in WWI era France, he falls in love with a quiet German woman he meets on a refugee train.
