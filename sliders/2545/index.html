Hannover Hauptbahnhof is one of the oldest stations in Germany. Although the interior has been extensively renovated, the exterior is largely preserved in its original condition. Source: Author.
A unique shopping promenade at level -1 extends from Hannover HBF into the city. Source: Author.
The promenade is just over 1/4 mile in length from the HBF to Kropke Plaza. Source: Author.
The promenade terminates at Kropke Plaza. Source: Author.
On the ground level, traffic is limited to pedestrians and bicycles. The lower space is relatively narrow with several stairways, escalators, and elevators along its length. Source: Author.
The station was only recently connected to the promenade during renovations for the Expo. Source: Author.
